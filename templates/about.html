All the hospital level data are from data.cms.gov, which are based on Inpatient Public Use File for Medicare. The total number of hosptials included is more than 3000. The data were updated on Aug 2019 and covers Fiscal Year 2017.

A state level map of average cost for different diagnosis is showed in order to potentially help patients to determine the ideal location to have treatment. The K-means algorithm is utilized here to further categorize hospitals into three clusters. Despite the factor that patients might have different insurance providers and plans, this project aims to help people, especially those don't have health insurance, to make better decisions when seeking treatment from hosptials.
